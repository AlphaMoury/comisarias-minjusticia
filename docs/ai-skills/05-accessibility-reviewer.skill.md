# Skill 05 — Accessibility and Pedagogy Reviewer

## Purpose

Review the deck so it is understandable, readable, and useful for non-technical officials.

## Checklist

- No slide has more than 3 bullets.
- No diagram has more than 9 nodes.
- Legal/technical terms are explained.
- The font size should be presentation-friendly.
- The deck works even if screenshots or animations fail.
- Every demo uses fictitious or anonymized information.
- Every legal draft includes human review warning.
- Speaker notes explain the technical parts simply.

## Red flags

- Too much text.
- Too many acronyms.
- Architecture too complex.
- Suggesting that AI makes legal decisions.
- Suggesting that free AI tools are safe for sensitive data.
