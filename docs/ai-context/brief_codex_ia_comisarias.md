# Brief — Taller IA para Comisarías de Familia

## Title

IA para Comisarías de Familia: del uso informal al copiloto institucional seguro

## Duration

2 hours.

## Audience

Officials from Comisarías de Familia, including legal, psychosocial, and administrative profiles.

## Workshop purpose

Explain how AI can support daily work in Comisarías de Familia while protecting sensitive data and preserving human legal judgment.

## Real operational pains to address

- Manual monitoring of deadlines and terms.
- Lack of unified national systems and interoperability.
- Coexistence of physical and digital records.
- Difficulty finding whether a child, family, or case has open proceedings in another entity.
- Informal use of free AI accounts with sensitive information.
- Poor-quality transcription of virtual hearings.
- Need to draft minutes, acts, preliminary autos, checklists, and reports.
- Format fragmentation.
- Difficulty generating genograms in a legally safe way.
- Unequal access to tools across municipalities and entities.

## Narrative arc

1. AI is already present, but informally.
2. The issue is not whether to use AI, but how to use it safely.
3. AI does not magically access private systems.
4. A chatbot is not the same as an institutional copilot.
5. Agents are AI systems with role, rules, tools, memory, and evaluation.
6. Soon, officials will build small applications without programming.
7. The institutional future is secure, traceable, interoperable, and human-reviewed.

## Demos

1. Anonymization.
2. Hearing transcript to act draft.
3. Deadline traffic-light prioritization.
4. Bad legal prompt vs safe legal prompt.
